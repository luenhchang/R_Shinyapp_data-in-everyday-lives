This webpage automatically analyses my Strava activities. To analyse your own data, first log into your Strava account on the Strava web service and perform a Bulk Export. Strava will then send you a link via email to download the export. This export contains all your uploaded data and comes in a large .zip file. The file you need to upload is activities.csv, which contains all your activity records.
